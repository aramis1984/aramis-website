Sports on the Terrace

The match, the mountains,
your drink.

Two large screens on the terrace show all major sports on demand — football, basketball, tennis, and whatever else is worth watching. When there’s an international billiards tournament, we put that up too.

The White Mountains stay in the background regardless. Settle in, order something good, and watch from one of the best rooftops in Chania.

Year-round

The view doesn’t change.
Neither does the welcome.

The terrace was renovated to work in every season — not just the ones with good weather. Here’s how it looks across the year.

☀
Summer
The sliding glass walls open fully — both halves of the terrace become one expansive space. Open sky, mountain view, warm evenings. The terrace at its most alive.
❄
Winter
The enclosed side comes into its own. Heated, glass-walled, solid roof — the White Mountains look exactly the same through the glass as they do in open air. Warm inside, spectacular outside.
