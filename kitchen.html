The Kitchen

From our kitchen
to your table.

"Food with soul, by the hands of Sissi and Dimitra."

The pizza is the reason people come back. Made fresh every time. The menu runs from early bites through sandwiches, burgers, pasta, and salads — all the way to sweet treats worth saving room for. Nine categories, everything prepared when you order.

This isn't bar food. Come for a game, stay for a meal — or the other way around.

The Bar

Coffee at ten.
Cocktails at ten.

"The bar opens when we do and doesn't slow down."

Morning espresso through to late-night cocktails — every hour of the day has something. Coffee, cold brews, fresh juices, milkshakes, soft drinks, beers on tap and by the bottle, a considered wine list, and a spirits shelf that earns its space.

The cocktail programme is where the bar really shows its character. Each drink is built with intention — and the names give you a hint before you've taken the first sip.
