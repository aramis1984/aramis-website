The Club

More than billiards.
An all day entertainment for all.

ARAMIS is where a serious game of pool meets a great kitchen and a bar that knows what it's doing. You come for the tables — you stay for everything else.

Crete's largest billiard hall. Ten professional tables. A kitchen with soul and a bar with personality — open from 10am to 1am. A terrace with a view worth the drive.
