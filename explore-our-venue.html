The Venue

The current ARAMIS opened in 2021 — the most complete version of the club since Aris first opened the doors in 1984. Ten professional billiard tables, a darts area, a rooftop terrace with White Mountains views, and a kitchen and bar that give people reasons to stay long after the last frame.

ARAMIS Culture
A place built on respect
for the game.

Forty years of billiards in Chania. The hall is quiet, focused, and fair — because our players make it that way. ARAMIS has always been a place where the game comes first and the people around you are given the same respect you'd want for yourself.
