What to expect

Not just billiards.
Not just a bar.

ARAMIS hosts live music, DJ sets, dance groups, themed nights, and seasonal celebrations throughout the year. Some events are planned weeks in advance; others come together because the evening calls for it.

The terrace is the stage for most of it — mountain views, open air, and space for everyone. In winter, we bring the energy indoors. Whether it's a Friday acoustic set or a full Saturday DJ night, the spirit is the same: good company, good drinks, and a venue that knows how to set the mood.
