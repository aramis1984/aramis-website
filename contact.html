On-site Parking
We have parking directly beneath the venue — first floor level, right below the club. Free and convenient.
Kladissos Parking
The Kladissos bridge municipal car park is free and approximately a 5-minute walk from the club. A good option when on-site is full.
From the Old Town
About 10–15 minutes on foot from Chania's Old Town. Frequent electric buses also connect central Chania to the area — a car-free option.
